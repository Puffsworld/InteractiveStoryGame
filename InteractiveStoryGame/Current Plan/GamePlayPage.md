# GamePlayPage.md

- [ ] **GamePlay Animation**: Implement a smooth animation when the user clicks on any choice button and move to the next scene.

The Best Transition (The "Cozy" Vibe)
Since your goal is a "cozy" and "cinematic" feel (Studio Ghibli style), you want to avoid "sliding" transitions (which feel like a PowerPoint) or "flips" (which feel like a card game).

Recommendation: The "Dissolve & Float" This mimics a dream-like state or a movie scene change.

The Image: A slow Cross Dissolve (approx. 0.5s - 0.8s).

The old scene fades out while the new one fades in. It feels soft and immersive.

The Text: A subtle Fade In + Move Up.

The text shouldn't appear instantly. It should fade in slightly after the image starts appearing (maybe a 0.2s delay).

Adding a slight "slide up" (moving 10 pixels up as it fades in) makes it feel elegant.
